Kettlebells

Kettlebells is a collection of nft collectibles. Each kettlebell has a physical equivalent in the real world. The physical kettlebells were gifted to me by my supervisor for every full paper published during my PhD at the University of Oulu, Finland, from 2018 - 2021. In March 2021, the physical kettlebells were buried or placed around Helsinki, Finland.
